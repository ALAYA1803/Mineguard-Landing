In modern open-pit mining operations, high-tonnage autonomous vehicles operate on defined routes with speeds and loads that make a conventional emergency stop impossible. When a passenger-operated light vehicle invades these routes, the risk of collision is immediate and potentially fatal.

Our proposal is primarily aimed at open-pit mining companies that operate autonomous fleets, as well as drivers of light vehicles who interact with these routes. For companies, Vertex represents a solution that reduces accidents, prevents economic losses due to downtime, and strengthens compliance with safety standards. For drivers, it provides an intuitive tool that improves their awareness of their surroundings without causing distractions.

Mission

Provide IoT-based technological solutions that enable the prevention of accidents in mining environments through real-time monitoring and early warning systems, protecting the lives of workers and optimizing operational safety.

⛏
Vision

Become the leading smart security solution for autonomous mining operations globally, driving the digital transformation of the sector towards safer, more efficient and reliable environments.
